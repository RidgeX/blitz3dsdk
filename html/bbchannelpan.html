bbChannelPan channel,pan#

Arguments
channelvalid playback channel
pan#left right stereo position
Description

Position the output of an audio channel in left right stereo space.

ValueEffective pan
0.0Left
0.25Center Left
0.5Center
0.75Center Right
1.0Right

Panning the position of sound effects in a video game is a useful technique for adding to the immersive experience.
